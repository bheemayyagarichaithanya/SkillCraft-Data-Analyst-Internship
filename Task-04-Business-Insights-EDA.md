# Task 04 – Business Insights Report (EDA) 📝

## Objective

Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a marketing campaign dataset and generate actionable business insights.

## Activities Performed

* Cleaned and prepared data
* Conducted exploratory data analysis
* Visualized marketing funnel performance
* Evaluated campaign ROI
* Generated business recommendations

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib / Seaborn

## Key Insights

* Identified high-performing marketing channels
* Compared campaign effectiveness
* Recommended budget allocation strategies based on ROI

## Deliverables

* EDA Report
* Visualizations
* Business Recommendation Summary
